A sentence is a group of words which forms a meaningful sense. A sentence is formed by following the grammatical rules of syntax. Grammatical rules vary from language to language.

Types of Sentences

  1. Declarative Sentence, or declaration, is the most common type of sentence. It tells something.
  2. Interrogative Sentence, or question, asks something.
  3. Exclamatory Sentence, or exclamation, says something out of the ordinary.
  4. Imperative Sentence, or command, tells someone to do something.
